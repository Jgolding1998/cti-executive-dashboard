Prediction Algorithm Details
How the forecast is calculated:
• Historical Patterns: Weighted average of sales by day of week (60%)
• AR Expectations: Expected collections from aging buckets (40%)
• Collection Rates: Current 20%/wk, 1-30d 15%/wk, 31-60d 10%/wk
• Actual Data: Days already passed use actual GL data
• Weekend Rule: Saturday/Sunday sales roll into Monday
• Confidence: Decreases with forecast distance

Formula: (Historical DOW Avg × 0.70) + (AR Collection Expectation × 0.30)
